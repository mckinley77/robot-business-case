The Real Value of Robotic Mowing
The greenkeeper capacity released is not treated as a financial saving. It represents skilled staff time that can be redirected towards drainage improvements, tree and woodland management, bunker maintenance, irrigation, presentation and other higher-value improvement work. Its annual value is shown separately and is not added to the cash saving.

Benefits Not Included in the Financial Comparison

  • Skilled greenkeeper capacity released for course improvement work
  • Reduced soil compaction and rutting on wet fairways
  • Ability to maintain selected areas during prolonged wet weather when heavier machinery would risk damage
  • More time for drainage, bunkers, presentation and detail work
  • Reduced diesel use and emissions
  • Improved winter presentation and playability
